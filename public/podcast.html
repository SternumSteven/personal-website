What IS the KashCast?

Beginning in 2020, I created my podcast, "KashCast", which is meant to highlight creatives, their careers, and their passions. I've used this to talk to people across multiple disciplines, learn how their professions work, and I'll continue to use it to platform artists in the future.

Here's a link to my podcast's RSS feed.

If you're ever interested in being a guest, please feel free to reach out! I'm interested in having people from all varieties. You can find my email form in the about section of this website, or alternatively reach me at my email here.

I'm currently working on a side show where guests can talk more about their interests, or a specific topic, instead of being shackled to a review segment. When that is complete I will make a new post for that.
