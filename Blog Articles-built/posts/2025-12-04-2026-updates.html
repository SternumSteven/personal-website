2026 Updates

Hey there, I’m currently writing this as I juggle… a lot. I honestly apologize for the lack of consistent updates. Life has been pretty hectic. I do hope however, that everyone has been okay during this holiday season.

Let’s speedrun through things I have on my plate.

VIDEOS / ARTICLES

I want to get a Kamikaze Girls video essay done before year’s end. I have some people who are nice enough to check the script out and feedback, but this is still going to try to be a personal yet well researched project. I already have the movie (which you can buy here) and I just purchased a digital copy of the manga that I’ll also be reading.

Sadly, life is hectic so I don’t know when I’ll be able to get that big Initial D video done. I need to get the lead out with video making, but it will get done eventually. The aim of having articles on this site IS to get more of them out quicker and made into potential videos, after all.

I honestly have many articles I’ve wanted to finish up but I’m too much of a perfectionist and it’s stopped me from getting all of them done. I’m trying to write more about some nerd stuff as well as tech habits, anime, or whatever else. Hopefully that’ll lead to a new opportunity, but I won’t count my eggs before they hatch.

PODCAST

I’m trying to get a new side show format off the ground, Extra Kash, or KashCast B-Sides maybe? Basically, people will be invited on to talk about a specific topic rather than following the interview format that lets people primarily talk about their jobs and backgrounds. I recorded one with Buster Corp about the future of Super Sentai, but with the current treatment of Maya Imamori it feels a bit tone deaf to talk about. Nobody could have seen it coming, but it’s affecting how we navigate talking about toku for a while. At the very least, I’ll wait until the block’s less hot so to speak. I do have a 12 minute clip of the episode where we talk about non Toei tokusatsu recommendations, and will upload that as soon as I can.

For the main show, I still have to edit the episode I did with Nick Cruz, one of the color designers on Kiff, the show I work on during the day. Nick is a great guy who’s immensely talented in his own right and we naturally click because of having a huge amount of interests being shared. I do have an episode with Justin Sevakis, CEO of MediaOCD and the original creator of Anime News Network, locked in but we haven’t recorded yet.

WEBSITE

As I write this in LibreOffice, I’m currently trying to get used to Neocities again. In order to move off of ghost, this means I will have to re-write my articles in order to have them show up on my Neocities. As of now, my ghost site is still online, but I need more control and ONLY having my credit card be a payment option is a no-go.

The new website will have easy to find links to The Animation Production Survival Guide and I’ll also reach out to other resources I’ve found, or other people I know have made. I strongly believe in people being able to share resources and knowledge. You can also look forward to guest profiles on the podcast, and maybe a gallery of all of the album art we’ve used?

ACTIVISM

I haven’t been able to do as much work as I’d like, but I’m currently a member of the TAG Health and Accommodations Group. It’s made by a lot of wonderful people who are trying to raise awareness and fight for change in the workplace, rallying around causes to make sure both in-office and remote workers are safe and have representation, while making sure everyone is informed and can advocate for themselves as well as their peers, especially regarding COVID.

If you’re interested in learning more, feel free to click here, and join our server if you work in animation! https://linktr.ee/tag839haa

I can’t do everything, of course. But I make an active effort to advocate for my fellow peers as well, especially with the state of the industry not being the best, and people having a tough time in general this year. It’s important to help the people you care about while staying politically informed.

https://www.project2025.observer/en

Do your part to call PayPal as well because payment processors are actively harming queer and NSFW creators and the ability to make art or media that is even slightly transgressive is actively being affected.

Stay informed here: https://anti-censorship-campaign.carrd.co/

ART / MY OWN CREATIONS

I hope that 2026 is a better year to get my web comic, “Fragments” off the ground. As I write this, the very real fact that I work on too many projects is starting to sink in, but I’m going to try to get a better mindset about my art and learn more about creating in order to make something out of it. With luck I can code an independent site about it as well?

I’m helping an art collective made by my friend Venchii, and I’ll update this space about that as well. I’m learning the hard way though that I WILL be fatigued if I don’t also take care of myself, so I’m going to be more proactive in taking breaks and making things for myself instead of ONLY making things for others.

There’s also no set schedule for anything I’m making especially since due to my inconsistent schedule I haven’t made much money off of it. I’ll update my site in case one does want to support me, but don’t feel obligated.

Thanks for being patient with me this year, I’ve faced a lot of struggles and continue to face them but I’m going to try to keep my head up. Here’s to 2026!
